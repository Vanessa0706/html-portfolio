The Best Movies According to Vanessa

My top 3 movies of all-time

You've Got Mail

This is my favorite movie to watch in the fall. It has the essence of reading, leaves changing colors in New York City, and a love story

Something From Tiffany's

I love Zoey Deutch's character in this movie. Her character has great spirit and is comforting to watch while she cooks and/or bakes.

50 First Dates

Adam Sandler and Drew Barrymore in this comedic movie never fails to make me laugh.
